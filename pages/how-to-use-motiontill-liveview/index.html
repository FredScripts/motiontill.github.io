How To Use Motiontill Liveview

Easily use Motiontill LiveView and organize assets for Premiere Pro, After Effects, Illustrator, and Photoshop. Import files, create folders, add previews, and export packages with ease.

How to Use Motiontill LiveView: A Step-by-Step Guide

Get Started with Motiontill LiveView
After installing the .zxp file, Use Motiontill Liveview through Window > Extensions > Motiontill LiveView in Premiere Pro and After Effects. The extension is dockable, allowing you to resize the panel or maximize it using the ~ key for a better view.

Adding and Organizing Your Items

To add items to your project, simply right-click on the center of the LiveView panel or click the “+” icon in the bottom corner. You can also create folders by hitting the folder icon next to the “+” sign. This makes it easy to keep your assets organized and accessible.

Import Items Seamlessly with Motiontill LiveView for After Effects and Premiere Pro

Motiontill LiveView makes managing your creative assets easier than ever by allowing you to import a wide range of files directly into the extension. Whether you’re working in Premiere Pro or After Effects, you can quickly organize and access all your essential assets in one place.

Supported Importable Files in Premiere Pro:

  • Image Files: PNG, JPG
  • Video Files: MP4, MOV
  • Project Files: PRPROJ
  • Templates: MOGRT files
  • Scripts: JSX and JSXBIN files

Supported Importable Files in After Effects:

  • Image Files: PNG, JPG
  • Video Files: MP4, MOV
  • Project Files: AET, AEP
  • Presets: FFX files
  • Scripts: JSX and JSXBIN files

With Motiontill LiveView, you can easily manage and import your files, ensuring quick access and a streamlined workflow. Organize your assets, create previews, and get to work faster with seamless file support for both After Effects and Premiere Pro.

Each item in the LiveView panel is marked with an icon showing its type, along with two key features:

  • Star Icon (): Bookmark your favorite items for quick access in the bookmarks section.
  • Info Icon (“i”): Add detailed information about your items, including HTML text, links, and extra descriptions.

Manage Your Items

By right-clicking on any imported or created item, you can:

  • Rename the item directly in the Motiontill LiveView extension.
  • Set picture and video previews to make it easier to identify your assets.
  • Drag and drop items into folders to keep everything organized.

Creating and Exporting Packages

Use Motiontill Liveview allows you to create and export custom packages easily:

  1. Right-click on any folder at the root of the LiveView panel and select Create Package.
  2. Add your name as the package creator and include a link to contact your audience.
  3. Once collected, you’ll receive a .liveview file and an asset folder containing all the renamed items with their associated picture and video previews.

Additionally, your package will include:

  • Help Folder: Contains an HTML file with instructions on how to import the package.
  • Catalog.html: A catalog file that displays all items within the package, allowing your audience to preview them before importing them into LiveView.
Optimize Your Workflow

Motiontill LiveView is designed to simplify and speed up the process of managing and exporting your creative assets for After Effects, Premiere Pro, Illustrator, and Photoshop. With features like easy bookmarking, detailed item information, and seamless package creation, you can efficiently organize and share your assets with your audience.
