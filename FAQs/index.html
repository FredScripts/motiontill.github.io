Frequently Asked Questions about Motiontill LiveView

Is Motiontill LiveView free to use?

Yes, Motiontill LiveView is completely free for users! You can enjoy all its features without any subscription or hidden fees, making it an ideal choice for enhancing your Adobe CC workflow.

What is Motiontill LiveView used for?

Motiontill LiveView is an extension designed for Adobe After Effects and other Adobe Creative Cloud software. It helps you manage files and packages, with features like picture and video previews, HTML guides for each package item, and more.

Which Adobe apps are compatible with Motiontill LiveView?

Motiontill LiveView works seamlessly with After Effects, but it is also compatible with other Adobe CC apps like Premiere Pro and Illustrator, making it a versatile tool for various creative workflows.

How does Motiontill LiveView improve file management?

Motiontill LiveView provides a clear, organized view of all your files with previews for images and videos. It also generates HTML guides for each package item, helping you quickly find and manage your assets within Adobe software.

What makes Motiontill LiveView unique?

Motiontill LiveView uses a persistent websocket connection powered by a lightweight Elixir process. This ensures real-time, synchronous communication between client and server, delivering fast and responsive file management, which is essential for professional video and animation work.

Can I get support if I face issues with Motiontill LiveView?

Yes, we offer support for Motiontill LiveView users. If you encounter any issues, feel free to contact us through the support section on our website, and our team will assist you promptly.

Do I need any special hardware to use Motiontill LiveView?

No special hardware is required. As long as you have a compatible Adobe CC software installed on your system, you can use Motiontill LiveView without any additional setup.

How do I install Motiontill LiveView?

Installation is simple. Just download the extension from our website, and follow the instructions to integrate it with your Adobe software. Once installed, you can access it directly from within your Adobe apps.
